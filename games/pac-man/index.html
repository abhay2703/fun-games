👾 Pac-Man

Score: 0 Best: 0 Lives: 3

📖 How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to navigate. Eat all dots to complete the level. Avoid ghosts — eat power pellets to chase them!

  • Arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Eat all dots to win
  • Large dots = power pellets
  • 3 lives per game

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Pac-Man improves reaction time, strategic planning, and spatial navigation.

  • Reflex: Dodge ghosts quickly
  • Strategy: Plan safe routes
  • Spatial: Navigate the maze

Recommended for: All ages (6+)
