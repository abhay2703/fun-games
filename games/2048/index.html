🔢 2048

Score: 0 Best: 0

📖 How to Play

Use arrow keys or swipe to slide all tiles. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one. Reach 2048 to win!

  • Arrow keys or swipe to slide
  • Same numbers merge when they collide
  • A new tile appears after each move
  • Game ends when no moves remain

🎯 Skills & Benefits

2048 enhances mathematical thinking, strategic planning, and spatial reasoning.

  • Strategy: Plan several moves ahead
  • Math: Work with powers of 2
  • Spatial: Manage grid positioning

Recommended for: Ages 8+

❓ FAQ

What happens after I reach 2048?
You can keep playing to achieve higher tiles and scores!
Is there a strategy?
Keep your highest tile in a corner and build a chain of decreasing values.
Can I undo a move?
No undo is available — think carefully before each move!
