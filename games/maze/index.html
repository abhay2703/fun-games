📖 How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to navigate from the green start to the red finish.

  • Arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Swipe on mobile
  • Green = Start, Red = Finish
  • Find the path through the maze

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Maze Runner enhances spatial navigation and visual-spatial processing.

  • Spatial: Navigate complex paths
  • Focus: Track position and direction
  • Strategy: Plan routes ahead

Recommended for: All ages (5+)
