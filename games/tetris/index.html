🟦 Block Stack

Score: 0 Lines: 0 Best: 0

📖 How to Play

Use arrow keys to move and rotate falling pieces. Complete horizontal lines to clear them.

  • ← → Move left/right
  • ↑ Rotate piece
  • ↓ Soft drop (faster)
  • Space: Hard drop (instant)

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Block Stack develops spatial reasoning, quick decision-making, and pattern recognition.

  • Spatial: Rotate and place pieces optimally
  • Reflex: Quick placement decisions
  • Strategy: Plan for upcoming pieces

Recommended for: All ages (6+)
