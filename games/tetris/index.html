🟦 Block Stack

📖 How to Play

Stack falling blocks to complete horizontal lines.

  • ← → Move left/right
  • ↑ Rotate piece
  • ↓ Soft drop (faster)
  • Space: Hard drop (instant)
  • Complete a line to clear it
  • 4 lines at once = big bonus
