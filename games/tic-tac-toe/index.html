⭕ Tic Tac Toe

Score: 0 Best: 0 Wins: 0

📖 How to Play

Click on any empty cell to place your mark (X). You play against the computer (O). Get three marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win!

  • You play as X, computer plays as O
  • Take turns placing marks
  • First to get 3 in a row wins
  • If all cells fill up with no winner, it's a draw

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Tic Tac Toe develops strategic thinking, pattern recognition, and the ability to anticipate your opponent's moves.

  • Strategic Thinking: Plan moves ahead
  • Pattern Recognition: Spot winning combinations
  • Decision Making: Choose optimal moves under constraints

Recommended for: All ages (5+)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play against another person?
Currently the game is player vs computer. The AI provides a moderate challenge.
Is there a strategy to always win?
The first player can guarantee at least a draw with perfect play by starting in the center or a corner.
What happens when the board is full?
If neither player gets three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
