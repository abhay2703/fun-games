⭕ Tic Tac Toe

📖 How to Play

Click on any empty cell to place your mark (X). You play against the computer (O). Get three marks in a row to win!

  • You play as X, computer plays as O
  • Take turns placing marks
  • First to get 3 in a row wins
  • If all cells fill up — it's a draw
