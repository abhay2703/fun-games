💣 Minesweeper

Mines: 10 Time: 0s

📖 How to Play

Left-click to reveal a cell. Numbers show how many adjacent cells contain mines. Right-click or use Flag Mode to mark suspected mines.

  • Click to reveal cells
  • Numbers = adjacent mine count
  • Right-click or Flag Mode to flag mines
  • Reveal all safe cells to win

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Minesweeper develops logical deduction, probability assessment, and systematic problem-solving.

  • Logic: Deduce mine locations
  • Strategy: Choose safe cells systematically
  • Pattern Recognition: Read number patterns

Recommended for: Ages 8+

❓ FAQ

How many mines are there?
The beginner board has 10 mines on a 9×9 grid.
What does the number mean?
Each number tells you how many of the 8 surrounding cells contain mines.
Can the first click be a mine?
No! The first click is always safe.
