💣 Minesweeper

📖 How to Play

Reveal cells to find all safe squares. Numbers show adjacent mine count.

  • Click to reveal a cell
  • Numbers = adjacent mine count
  • Right-click to flag suspected mines
  • Or use Flag Mode button
  • First click is always safe
  • Reveal all safe cells to win
Mines: 10 Time: 0s
