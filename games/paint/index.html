🎨 Paint & Draw

📖 How to Use

Click and drag to draw. Use the color picker and brush size controls below the canvas.

  • Click & drag to draw
  • Pick colors and brush sizes
  • Toggle eraser mode
  • Undo last stroke or clear all
  • Save your artwork as PNG

🎯 Skills & Benefits

Paint & Draw encourages creative expression, fine motor skills, and relaxation.

  • Creativity: Free-form expression
  • Motor Skills: Precise mouse/touch control
  • Relaxation: Stress-free creative outlet

Recommended for: All ages (3+)
